# Teaching Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Course

This comprehensive course covers the fundamentals to advanced concepts in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Designed for students, researchers, and hobbyists with varying technical backgrounds, the course provides both theoretical knowledge and practical applications.

## Course Overview

Physical AI represents a paradigm where artificial intelligence is fundamentally integrated with physical systems. This course explores how AI algorithms can be designed to work seamlessly with physical robots, particularly humanoid robots that interact with the physical world.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:
- Understand the fundamental principles of Physical AI and its applications in humanoid robotics
- Grasp the engineering challenges unique to embodied AI systems
- Learn how to design and implement AI algorithms that interact with the physical world
- Explore case studies of successful Physical AI implementations
- Develop skills in simulating and testing Physical AI systems

### Target Audience

This course is designed for:
- Students studying robotics, AI, or related fields
- Researchers exploring embodied AI systems
- Engineers working on robotics applications
- Hobbyists interested in humanoid robotics
- Anyone curious about the intersection of AI and physical systems

### Prerequisites

While the course is designed to accommodate different technical backgrounds, having the following will be helpful:
- Basic understanding of programming (especially Python)
- Fundamental knowledge of robotics concepts
- Familiarity with AI and machine learning basics
