# Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive course on Teaching Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics!

This course provides a deep exploration of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, covering both theoretical foundations and practical implementation aspects. The content is structured to accommodate students, researchers, and hobbyists with varying technical backgrounds.

## Course Overview

In this course, you will explore:

- **Physical AI Fundamentals**: Core principles of intelligence embodied in physical systems
- **Humanoid Design Principles**: Approaches to designing and controlling humanoid robots
- **Motor Control Systems**: Techniques for controlling humanoid movement
- **Sensor Fusion**: Integration of multiple sensor modalities for perception
- **Locomotion Algorithms**: Methods for bipedal walking and movement
- **AI for Physical Systems**: Application of AI techniques to physical robots
- **Control Theory Applications**: Implementation of control algorithms for humanoid systems
- **Case Studies & Applications**: Real-world implementations and use cases
